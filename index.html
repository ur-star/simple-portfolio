About Me

Hello There,
Hello, I’m Siddharth Roy, a dedicated civil engineer with a strong background in executing large-scale infrastructure projects. Over the years, I've honed my skills in structural engineering, project management, and team leadership, focusing on delivering quality results while optimizing resources and timelines. I graduated with a B.Tech in Civil Engineering from KUK University in 2019, and since then, I’ve had the privilege of working with some of the industry’s top construction companies. From my early days as a Site Engineer at Ojasindia Contract Pvt. Ltd., working on RE wall construction in Ghazipur, to my current role at S.P. Singla Construction Pvt. Ltd. managing bridge structures for the Vikramshila Setu Cable Stayed Bridge, I have been actively involved in executing and managing complex civil projects across a variety of domains. With hands-on experience in everything from pile foundations, pier shafts, and pile cap works to structural steel and TMT requirements, I’ve consistently demonstrated a commitment to accuracy, safety, and timely project delivery. I specialize in well foundation execution, BBS preparation, structural steel analysis, and site management, including billing and quantity analysis. My work has contributed to the successful completion of projects such as the Aligarh-Kanpur Expressway and the Vikramshila Setu bridge. Throughout my career, I’ve worked closely with diverse teams, always striving to foster collaboration and ensure that everyone—from laborers to senior engineers—is aligned with the project’s goals. I firmly believe that empathy, clear communication, and a strong work ethic are key to leading successful teams and delivering results. I’m constantly looking for new challenges and opportunities to apply my skills in innovative ways and contribute to the growth of the companies I work with. If you're interested in collaborating or want to learn more about my professional journey, feel free to get in touch!
